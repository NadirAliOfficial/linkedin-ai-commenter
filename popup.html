How to Use
  1. 1 Open your LinkedIn feed and scroll
  2. 2 Find the Quick Comment bar under any post
  3. 3 Pick a tone: Support, Insightful, Agree, Question, Congratulate, Challenge, Experience, or Add Value
  4. 4 Review the generated comment
  5. 5 Click "Insert Comment" to paste it in the box
  6. 6 Press LinkedIn's own Post button to publish
